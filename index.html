50 Surreal Adventures of Sentient Objects

Dive into the strange and beautiful world where everyday objects come alive and break the laws of physics! This stunning full-color art book features 50 micro-stories about sentient teacups, rebellious paperclips, philosophical houseplants, and more as they discover cosmic powers and travel between dimensions.

Each lavishly illustrated story includes:

  • Character profiles of unforgettable sentient objects
  • Full-page surreal artwork that defies imagination
  • Complete micro-film scripts for aspiring creators
  • Behind-the-scenes creative notes from the artists

Based on the viral YouTube series "Ten Seconds of Impossible™" with over 10 million views, this coffee table book is perfect for fans of surrealist art, animation enthusiasts, and anyone who loves to see reality bent in beautiful and unexpected ways.
